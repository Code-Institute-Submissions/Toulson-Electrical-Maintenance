About Us

Get to know Rob, owner and founder of Toulson Electrical Maintenance

Rob Toulson

  • Raised in southwest London, Rob Toulson knew from a young age he wanted to build and fix things. He took his passion to the next level and completed courses in many knowledge areas at a young age to be able to work as a certified professional in the industry.

  • His background includes electrical maintenance, fire & safety, and project management. He has over 20 years of experience in Building Services and Maintenance Manager roles in addition to completing countless projects for business and homes.

  • Rob works with his brother as well as a few carefully selected engineers to get your job done to the highest standard.
